Fence Panels   
 
              All our fence panels and trellis are manufactured on site , with a  
   choice of pressure treated feather edge, or oil based double sided to choose from.  
 
  Feather edge panels are made with 3" backing rails with 18 , 5" vertical boards as standard.
 
  Double sided panels are fully framed and are the same both sides as standard.  
 
  All panels and trellis are fitted with capping , including bowed and scalloped.  
 
  Fence panels can be made to any size to suit your requirements.  
 
  Feather edge gates , bowed and scalloped panels are made to order.
